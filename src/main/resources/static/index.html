General Rules

· Please be aware your Twitter, Facebook and Telegram account need firstly to be associated with bounty program before you start earning points (You can find the links on the menu bar)

· On top of the page is an URL for you to use, the URL will be used to invite people and to earn points.

· You can earn points by completing programs during the three phases of bounty program. You will get LOTS tokens within three (3) weeks following the conclusion of the third phase of this program, be transferred to the Ether address that you have provided.

· Every qualified participant would receive rewards in forms of LOTS tokens at the end of each phase of bounty program. In the first phase, the total prize pool is 2,000,000 LOTS tokens. Top 100 winners would receive rewards approximately 70% of the total prize pool. Below is the chart of rewards distributed to Top 100 winners.
